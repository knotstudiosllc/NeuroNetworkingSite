Neuro Networking, by design, allows developers to easily implement multiplayer for their VR game in unity!Why Should You Get It?NeuroNet is built to save you time and remove the complexity of multiplayer networking. Instead of fighting low-level systems, you get a streamlined wrapper that lets you focus on building your game. It provides secure, relay-based networking using FishNet and EOS, keeping player IPs protected while maintaining reliable connections across sessions. Designed with VR developers in mind, NeuroNet makes adding multiplayer to Unity projects faster, simpler, and more scalable, whether you are prototyping or preparing for a full release. If you want dependable networking without the headache, NeuroNet gives you the foundation you need to bring your multiplayer VR experience to life.
